Milestones

What is milestone. How to create the milestone. How to relate the milestones with your tasks. How many milestone open at time. Past (Previous week milestone in which all the task in a status completed and deployed) Current (The task on which you are working in current week) Ice freeze (All the task which you are planning to complete in future) On every Monday your Milestone will be changed. Past milestone must have all the task in completed and deployed status. Those task which are not completed in previous milestone will move in current milestone.
